International Business

About International Business:

International Business refers to any business activities that involves global-scale of trade and transaction of goods. Such transaction and activities can include operation, manufacture & distribution, research and development across the borders.

International Business Career Paths:

Management Analyst

Management Analyst analyze the company operation in both domesitc and oversea market. Their goal is to maximize the efficiency of the company's operation by adjusting the company's structure, minimizing cost and keeping productivity high. They also analyze finance data and interview employees. This occupation often involves significant travel or even living abroad for a long time of period.

Translator/Interpretor

Translator plays vitol role in the transaction of goods across the borders. Accurate translation is mandatory for the successful communication and trade and it is translator's job to keep the communication between two organization in flow.

International Economist

International Economists study the international economy of how countries and markets utilize resources to produce products or service. They analyze market's trend and make projection. Economists then provide a firm a market forecast across countries which helps management with decision.

Import/Export Coordinator

Import/Export Coordinator arrange communications between foreign/domestic customers and sales representative. They are also responsible for shipment, delivery, order approval, and invoices. This career is increasing in demand with expanding globalization.
