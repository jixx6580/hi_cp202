International Business

About International Business:

International Business refers to any business activities that involves global-scale of trade and transaction of goods. Such transaction and activities can include operation, manufacture & distribution, research and development across the borders.

International Business Career Paths:

Management Analyst

Management Analyst analyzes the company operation in both the domesitc and oversea markets. Their goal is to maximize the efficiency of the company's operation by adjusting the company's structure, minimizing cost and keeping productivity high. They also analyze financial data and interview employees. This occupation often involves significant travel or even living abroad for a long time.

Translator/Interpretor

Translator plays a major role in the transaction of goods across the borders. Accurate translation is mandatory for the successful communication and trade and it is the translator's job to keep the communication between two organizations in flow.

International Economist

International Economists study the international economy of how countries and markets utilize resources to produce products or service. They analyze market's trends and make a projection. Economists then provide a firm a market forecast across countries which helps management making future decisions.

Import/Export Coordinator

Import/Export Coordinators arrange communications between foreign/domestic customers and the sales representative. They are also responsible for shipment, delivery, order approval, and invoices. This career is increasing in demand with expanding globalization.
