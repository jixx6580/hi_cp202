Finance

About Finance:

Finance deals with the study of investments. It studies assets and liabilities under different risk situations to see whether its worthwhile. Finance assess whether an investment should be made into an asset or company or stock based on multiple factors. Finance is an important factor for any company because their stakeholders rely on it.

Finance Career Paths:

Stockbroker

A stock broker is like a real estate agent, but for shares in a company they buy and sell stocks from client to client for a commission. Stockbrokers in Canada must have completed the Canadian Securities Course, the Conduct & Practices Handbook and the 90-day investment advisor training program. After these three initial tests are complete there are multiple following tests including Wealth Management Essentials course and a compliance training program done every 3 years.

Financial Advisor

A financial advisor aids with client’s who need help and advice on their financial situation. There are 3 distinct fields in Canada which are Life Insurance, Securitas or mutual funds. Depending on the field, multiple tests must be passed to obtain a license.

Financial Analyst

A financial analyst works for a corporation and sells and buys investments with their best judgement for the company’s best interests. Their job is to increase the companies value by bringing up new and potential investments that will benefit the company well.
